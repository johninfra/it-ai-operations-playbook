# Purpose

This workflow uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to accelerate technical research, problem investigation, technology evaluation, and decision making.

The objective is to reduce research time, improve information gathering, and support more informed technical decisions across IT operations and cybersecurity teams.

---

# Business Problem

Technical teams regularly spend significant time researching:

- Software solutions
- Technical issues
- Security threats
- Vendor products
- Best practices
- Infrastructure designs

Common challenges include:

- Information overload
- Outdated resources
- Inconsistent research methods
- Duplicate research efforts
- Time constraints

AI can assist by organizing information, identifying patterns, and summarizing findings.

---

# Scope

This workflow applies to:

- IT Operations Research
- Cybersecurity Research
- Vendor Evaluation
- Product Comparisons
- Technical Troubleshooting
- Infrastructure Planning

- Process Improvement

# Research Methodology

## Step 1: Define Objective

Clearly identify the research goal.

Example:

```text
Evaluate endpoint protection solutions for a 500-user organization.
```

---

## Step 2: Define Research Questions

Examples:

```text
What features are required?

What are the implementation costs?

What security benefits are provided?

What are the operational requirements?
```

---

## Step 3: Gather Information

Collect information from:

- Vendor Documentation
- Technical Articles
- Industry Reports
- Security Advisories
- Product Documentation
- Internal Documentation

---

## Step 4: Analyze Findings

Evaluate:

- Features
- Benefits
- Risks
- Costs
- Complexity
- Support Requirements

---

## Step 5: Generate Summary

Create:

- Executive Summary
- Technical Summary
- Recommendations
- Risk Assessment

---

## Step 6: Validate Results

Verify:

- Technical accuracy
- Source reliability
- Current relevance
- Business applicability

## Objective

```text
Determine whether Microsoft Intune should be implemented for endpoint management.
```

## Research Questions

```text

What deployment effort is involved?

What security improvements are gained?
```

```text
Remote device configuration

Policy enforcement

Security compliance monitoring
```

```text
Licensing costs

Training requirements
```
